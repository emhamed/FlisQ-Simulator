Project Description

FlasQ is an innovative platform that combines quantum computing and artificial intelligence to revolutionize molecular simulations and drug discovery.

  • Core Idea: Harness the power of quantum computing (via IBM’s Quantum iCloud service) to simulate the wave-like behavior of electrons in complex molecules and integrate AI algorithms for enhanced predictive analysis.
  • Technological Approach: Solve the Schrödinger equation using quantum algorithms, apply machine learning for data analysis, and offer a user-friendly interface for professionals and students alike.
  • Target Audience: University researchers and pharmaceutical companies looking for advanced simulation tools that accelerate drug discovery while reducing R&D costs.
  • Business Model: Subscription-based access with tiered pricing for academic institutions and industry professionals.

Features & Technology

Interactive 3D Molecular Viewer

Render molecular structures in 3D using cutting-edge libraries like 3Dmol.js. Rotate, zoom, and visualize complex molecules dynamically.

AI-Powered Insights

Receive automated, context-aware analyses of molecular structures to predict binding affinities and off-target interactions.

Quantum Simulation

Leverage quantum algorithms to accurately model electron behavior and molecular interactions at the atomic level.

User-Friendly Interface

Designed for both experts and novices, with tooltips, documentation, and interactive demos that simplify complex simulations.
